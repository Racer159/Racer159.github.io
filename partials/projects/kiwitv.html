Kiwi TV

Kiwi TV is an application written for the Universal Windows Platform that allows the user to watch live streaming television from a variety of sources. It features 25+ built-in channels along with Twitch.tv, and UStream integration to provide a wide range of channel options. The app also takes advantage of the UWP platform, being available across PC, mobile, and Xbox. You can learn more here.
