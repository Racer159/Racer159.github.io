Project Atlas

Project Atlas was a project I worked on while I was an intern at Microsoft along with other fellow interns. The basic idea was to create a physical implementation of social media, and to see how far that implementation could travel. To accomplish this, we placed Windows Phones with custom software running on them inside a laser-cut enclosure with speakers, lights, and a custom PCB to drive everything off of the audio jack on the phone. This came together to create what we dubbed an "Explorer." We ran this experiment for a few months, and found that the Explorers travelled coast to coast, and some even migrated to Canada.
