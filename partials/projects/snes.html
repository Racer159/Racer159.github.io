Bluetooth SNES Controller

This project just had the simple goal of creating a Bluetooth SNES controller, similar to the one on the Adafruit Learning System. I chose this project because I wanted to learn more about the hardware side of things, and this was a good way to get my feet wet. I didn't just follow the directions though, as I wanted a sleeker look then the Adafruit controller (they used an external charger), so there were some spacing challenges I had to overcome, and I had to get creative with the snips in a few areas of the controller. Overall I ended up with a clean looking SNES controller, just without the cable.
